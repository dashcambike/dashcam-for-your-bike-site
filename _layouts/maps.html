Each of these reports has been sent to the City of {{page.cityname}} via 311. The map does not represent the most dangerous areas of the city; rather, it represents areas where the most hazards have been reported, and where better infrastructure would be most impactful.

Contribute to this map

  1. Download our app for Android or iPhone and record every ride.
  2. When you encounter a hazard in {{page.cityname}}, tap the screen to add a marker.
  3. When you finish your ride, return to the marker, hit Share, then hit Report To 311.
